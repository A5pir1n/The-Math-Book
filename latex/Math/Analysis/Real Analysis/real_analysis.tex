\section{Numerical Sequences and Series}

\begin{definition}
$\{ p_n \}$ is a sequence. The set of all points $p_n$ $(n = 1, 2, 3, ...)$ is the \textit{range} of $p_n$. The range of a sequence may be a finite set, or it may be infinite. 
\end{definition}
