General Remarks

Moving to a bigger space in which we keep track of everything that is relevant for the evolution of our system is called ‘working in phase space’: The phase space of a general nth order ODE is the space of all possible times, positions, and ﬁrst n − 1 derivatives of the position. For a ball in ﬂight we could take the phase space to simply be the position and velocity of the ball; more accurate treatment might include additional variables to keep track of how the ball is spinning etc. (TBM)
